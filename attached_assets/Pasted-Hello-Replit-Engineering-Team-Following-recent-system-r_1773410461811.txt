Hello Replit Engineering Team,

Following recent system reviews and engine validation cycles, we identified a structural limitation in the current Channel Selection Engine that must be addressed to preserve the strategic integrity of the MarketMind architecture.

The issue arises when persuasion models and channel types appear incompatible, causing the engine to classify multiple channels as rejected or low-fit. While technically accurate, this behavior creates a decision dead-end in scenarios where the correct solution is not channel rejection but funnel restructuring.

To address this, we require the implementation of a Funnel-Oriented Channel Resolution Layer that operates between the Persuasion Engine and the Channel Engine Decision Gate.

This layer must resolve persuasion–channel mismatches through multi-channel funnel design instead of binary rejection logic.

---

## Core Architectural Principle

Channels should not be evaluated solely as isolated execution environments.

They must also be evaluated as positions within a marketing funnel.

A channel that is incompatible as a conversion environment may still be optimal as an awareness entry channel.

Therefore the engine must shift from:

Single-channel decision logic

to

Funnel-aware channel orchestration.

---

## Required System Behavior

When the Channel Engine detects a persuasion incompatibility, the system must attempt funnel reconstruction before rejecting the channel.

Example scenario:

Persuasion Model  
Education-Led

Channel Candidate  
Short-form Social (Instagram / TikTok)

Current Behavior  
Channel Rejected

Required Behavior  

The system must convert the channel role to Attention Entry Point and route deeper persuasion through a secondary channel.

Example Funnel Output:

Attention Layer  
Instagram Reels / TikTok

Engagement Layer  
YouTube / Long-form Video / Landing Page

Conversion Layer  
Offer Page / Webinar / Product Funnel

This allows persuasion depth to occur in environments that support it.

---

## Funnel Role Classification

The Funnel Resolution Layer must classify channels into three functional roles:

1. Awareness Channels  
Channels optimized for reach and attention capture.

2. Nurture Channels  
Channels capable of supporting education, trust building, and persuasion.

3. Conversion Channels  
Channels designed for transactional commitment.

Each channel must be evaluated across these roles rather than using a single compatibility score.

---

## Decision Logic Requirements

If a persuasion-channel incompatibility is detected:

1. Attempt Funnel Reconstruction
2. Reassign the channel to the most appropriate funnel stage
3. Identify a compatible channel for the persuasion stage
4. Construct a multi-step funnel recommendation

Only if no viable funnel configuration exists should the channel be rejected.

---

## Engine Isolation Requirement

This Funnel Resolution Layer must not violate the MarketMind Master Plan Engine Isolation Rule.

Specifically:

- The Persuasion Engine defines persuasion structure.
- The Channel Engine evaluates channel capabilities.
- The Funnel Resolution Layer only assigns channel roles within the funnel structure.

No engine may override another engine’s core logic.

---

## Acceptance Criteria (Strict)

The implementation will only be accepted if all the following conditions are satisfied:

1. Channels are no longer rejected solely due to persuasion incompatibility.
2. The system attempts funnel reconstruction before rejection.
3. Each recommended channel must include an assigned funnel role (Awareness / Nurture / Conversion).
4. The Funnel Resolution Layer must not modify persuasion outputs.
5. Channel role assignments must respect channel capability constraints.
6. The system must log funnel reconstruction decisions for auditability.
7. Decision Gate must still enforce final strategy validation after funnel reconstruction.

---

## Expected Outcome

After this enhancement:

• Channel rejections will decrease.  
• Strategic recommendations will become more actionable.  
• The system will transition from channel selection to funnel orchestration.  
• MarketMind will better emulate the reasoning process of senior marketing strategists.

This update is essential for ensuring that MarketMind operates not merely as a channel evaluator but as a true strategic marketing operating system.

Please confirm once the Funnel-Oriented Resolution Layer is implemented and validated so we can proceed with architecture verification.

Hi Replit Team,

MIv3 is very close to being fully closed. Before we mark the Market Intelligence engine as finalized, please run the following final verification checklist.  
If any item is FAIL, please fix it and confirm.

---

### 1. Signals-Only Contract
Verify that MIv3 outputs signals and observations only.

Confirm that no prescriptive or strategy language appears anywhere in engine outputs (e.g., “focus”, “advantage”, “you should”, “recommend”, “strategy”, etc.).

If found, clean the output so it remains purely descriptive market observations.

---

### 2. Threat vs Opportunity Separation
Confirm that signals are logically categorized.

Signals such as:
- revival potential
- declining activity

should not appear as threats only.

Verify that the system distinguishes between:
• Threat Signals  
• Opportunity Signals

---

### 3. Data Sufficiency Guard (Minimum Data Check)
Since the minimum post coverage was reduced to 14 posts, confirm that the engine has a data sufficiency guard.

Check that MIv3 evaluates:
- posts analyzed
- comments analyzed
- competitors analyzed

If coverage is too weak, the engine should downgrade confidence instead of returning normal analysis.

---

### 4. Confidence / Signal Quality Validation
Confirm that the system evaluates signal quality using:
- signalNoiseRatio
- evidence coverage
- competitor coverage

If signal quality is low, the system should indicate reduced confidence in the signals.

---

### 5. Snapshot Integrity
Confirm the snapshot system enforces:

• Engine version compatibility  
• Snapshot completeness  
• Snapshot freshness validation  

Old or incompatible snapshots should not be reused.

---

### 6. Freshness Hard Gate
Confirm the 14-day freshness rule is enforced:

If analyzed data is older than 14 days, the engine should block or flag the analysis as stale.

---

### 7. Threat Signal Coverage
Verify that structural threat detection is active for major patterns such as:

- narrative convergence
- hook duplication
- offer clustering
- aggressive scaling
- market density changes
- positioning shifts
- declining activity
- revival potential

---

### 8. Market Trajectory Interpretations
Confirm that each trajectory index includes a signal interpretation line explaining the observed pattern, without turning it into advice.

---

### 9. Competitor Intelligence Completeness
Verify competitor cards expose structural signals such as:
- narrative pattern
- hook style
- CTA pattern
- content style

If insufficient post data exists, the UI should clearly indicate that.

---

### 10. Evidence Coverage Transparency
Confirm that MIv3 always exposes:

• posts analyzed  
• comments analyzed  
• competitors analyzed  
• signal quality (signalNoiseRatio)

so users understand the reliability of the signals.

---

### 11. Layer-0 Boundary Enforcement
MIv3 must remain pure Market Intelligence.

Confirm that it produces:
• signals  
• patterns  
• observations

and does not produce strategy recommendations or actions.

---

### 12. Test Coverage
Confirm that all regression tests pass and that checks exist for:
- signals-only output
- freshness hard gate
- snapshot integrity
- data sufficiency guard

---

Once this checklist is verified and any remaining gaps are corrected, MIv3 can be considered fully closed as the Market Intelligence layer.

Please confirm PASS/FAIL for each item.

Thanks.

Before launch, we need to fully lock down the AI Video (Veo) cost layer to prevent uncontrolled usage and protect margins.

This is critical — video generation must NOT behave like standard AI usage.

---

## 🎯 Core Decision (Must Be Enforced)

AI Video generation must be treated as a separate system, not part of the general AI token budget.

- ❌ Do NOT include video usage inside the existing token budget
- ❌ Do NOT allow unlimited or implicit video generation

- ✅ Video must run on a separate credit system

---

## 💳 1. Video Credits System

Introduce a simple credit model:

- Each video generation = 1 credit
- Credits are limited per user based on plan

### Initial structure:
- No dynamic pricing logic needed
- Just a fixed credit count per account

---

## 📦 2. Plan Allocation

Set initial limits:

- One Shot:
  - ❌ No video access

- Growth:
  - 2 video credits

- Ultra:
  - 5 video credits

---

## ➕ 3. Add-On Purchase (Simple)

Support basic top-up:

- Allow users to purchase additional video credits (via Stripe link)
- No complex billing system — just:
  - payment → increase credit count

---

## 🧠 4. Model Control (Important)

For now:
- Use Veo Fast only

Do NOT expose:
- Standard / high-cost variants yet

Later we can introduce:
- “High Quality Mode” as a premium option

---

## 🧩 5. UX Behavior

Update UI:

- Replace:
  → “Generate Video”

- With:
  → “Generate Video (1 credit)”

When credits are exhausted:
- Show:
  → “You’ve used all your video credits”
  → CTA: Buy more / Upgrade

---

## ⚠️ 6. Hard Constraints

- Must enforce limits at backend level (not UI only)
- No way to bypass credits
- No silent generation without credit deduction

---

## ✅ 7. Validation Checklist

Before marking complete, confirm:

1. Video generation consumes 1 credit per request
2. Users cannot generate video without credits
3. Plan-based credit allocation works correctly
4. Add-on credit increases are reflected immediately
5. No dependency on AI token budget
6. Only Veo Fast is used

---

## 🎯 Goal

- Fully isolate video cost from core AI system
- Prevent margin loss from heavy users
- Keep implementation simple and controlled

---

Once this is done, the cost structure will be fully secured for launch.

We need to resolve a structural gap in the Market Intelligence engine that became visible during the recent Persuasion Engine diagnostics. The issue is not a failure of the Persuasion Engine itself — the root cause is that the Market Intelligence pipeline is not currently extracting market objections from the available data sources.

Important clarification about our system architecture:

Our platform does not scrape or process Instagram comments, and it must remain that way. The system only analyzes:

• Post captions  
• Hook lines  
• CTA language  
• Narrative framing  
• Competitor comparison statements  
• Positioning language within posts  

Therefore, the objection extraction solution must be built entirely from caption-level narrative analysis, not from comments.

---

### 1. Implement a Caption-Level Objection Extraction Layer (Inside MI Only)

Add a new internal module within the Market Intelligence engine called:

Narrative Objection Extraction Layer

This module must detect objections indirectly through narrative patterns in captions and hooks. Many markets reveal objections through the way competitors frame their messaging, even when objections are not explicitly written.

Examples of linguistic signals to detect:

Comparison patterns
• “Unlike most agencies…”  
• “Most agencies focus on vanity metrics…”  

Anti-pattern framing
• “Stop wasting money on agencies that…”  
• “Why most agencies fail their clients.”  

Trust repair language
• “Tired of agencies overpromising results?”  
• “What agencies don’t tell you about growth.”

Problem framing language
• “The reason most businesses struggle with marketing…”  

These patterns reveal market objections indirectly and should be captured as objection signals.

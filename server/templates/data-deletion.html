Data Deletion Instructions

Avyron AI

How to Request Data Deletion

We respect your privacy and your right to control your personal data. If you would like to delete your data from Avyron AI, you can do so using any of the methods below.

Option 1: In-App Deletion

1

Open the Avyron AI app and go to Settings.

2

Scroll down and tap Delete My Account.

3

Confirm your identity and tap Delete to permanently remove your account and all associated data.

Option 2: Email Request

Send an email to our support team with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and include the email address or user ID associated with your account.

Send Deletion Request

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon receiving your deletion request, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your account profile information (name, email, profile picture)
  • Facebook and Instagram login credentials and tokens
  • Generated content, designs, and media files
  • Campaign data and scheduling history
  • Brand profiles and platform connection settings
  • Analytics and usage history

Processing Time: Deletion requests are processed within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation once your data has been fully removed.

Facebook & Instagram Data

If you logged in using Facebook or Instagram, you can also remove Avyron AI's access to your data directly from your Meta account:

  1. Go to Facebook Settings > Apps and Websites
  2. Find Avyron AI in the list of active apps
  3. Click Remove to revoke access and delete data shared with our app

This will remove any data that Facebook shared with Avyron AI through the login process.
